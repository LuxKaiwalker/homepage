About the competition

  • (they do not have a website)
  • Target: Skilled german high schools students interested in competitive programming, mainly selected from prestigeous german high school competitions.
  • Rounds: 3. First round is the so called pre selection, second round is the actual selection process and the third round, consisting of the top 6-8 people, were ultimately selected for the IOI team. The further you make it in the selction, the more training you will receive.
  • Result and Year: Ranked 16th in the second selection round and did not made it to the final round.
